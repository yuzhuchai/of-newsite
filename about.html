OF COURSE
想当然
创立于2014年，中国第一家元宇宙科技学院。核心成员来自世界顶尖高校，为18-48岁成年人提供下一代互联网生存所需的视野与技能。 Founded in 2014, OF Course is China's first Metaverse Institute of science and technology.
想当然，是一个具有国际影响力的元宇宙科技学院。我们与全球顶尖教授、新媒体艺术家共同研发“OF 创意编程课程”，提供最前沿的教学指导，让学员从零起步成长为创作先锋。

作为一个高速增长的元宇宙品牌，想当然聚集了50,000名来自新媒体艺术、人工智能、互联网、科研、风投等领域人才。我们的合作伙伴包括美国帕森斯设计学院、同济大学、WildAid、阿里巴巴、索尼、Frog、TBWA和W+K等机构。

我们始终倡导艺术、文化和科技的跨界融合，为企业提供人才，为大众传播前沿资讯，也为客户提供个性化品牌创意服务。 Of course, it is a metaverse science and technology college with international influence. We have jointly developed the "OF Creative Programming Course" with the world's top professors and new media artists, providing cutting-edge teaching guidance, allowing students to grow from zero to creative pioneers.

As a fast-growing metaverse brand, it has naturally gathered 50,000 talents from new media art, artificial intelligence, Internet, scientific research, venture capital and other fields. Our partners include Parsons School of Design, Tongji University, WildAid, Alibaba, Sony, Frog, TBWA and W+K.
